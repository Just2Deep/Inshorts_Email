The special session of the Parliament has begun and the first day will mark the 75 years of the parliamentary journey. PM Narendra Modi addressed the media ahead of the special session of the Parliament and said that historic decisions will be taken in this session. 3 women buried alive in crater after land subsidence at BCCL coal mine in Dhanbad :

Three women were buried alive after falling into a crater that developed due to land subsidence in a colliery area of coal company Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) in Jharkhand's Dhanbad on Sunday. Eyewitnesses said one of the women slipped into a 30-foot crater that developed suddenly and the two others fell into it while trying to rescue her.

Indian Army soldier on leave abducted & shot dead in Manipur :

An Indian Army soldier was abducted from his home and shot dead by unidentified armed men in Manipur on Sunday, officials said. The soldier, identified as Sepoy Serto Thangthang Kom, was on leave. According to his 10-year-old son, the only eyewitness to the crime, the accused placed a pistol on his head and forced him into a white vehicle.
